Hello World Weather Station

This is my first serious Arduino project, and I wanted to make something that I kinda really need for my homesteading projects. So I'm starting small (it's a $20 digital thermometer with humidity and an LCD screen to tizzy it up) but there's an eventual aim of making it much more capable, and also capable of being part of a network of monitoring stations for all sorts of other things.

Milestone Reached 29/07/15
